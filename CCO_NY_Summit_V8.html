🔑 Champion A — Opener
CCO, Chief Customer Officer, Chief Client Officer, Chief Experience Officer. Direct access to the real DM (CEO/CFO). They don't buy, but one word from them kills or accelerates any evaluation. Goal: turn them into internal sponsors.
Approach: executive — strategic positioning content, no demo.
⚙️ Champion B — Evaluator
VP/EVP in any cluster + operational clusters (CX Ops, Support, Ops, Product). Seniority elevates to B — carry decision weight and DM access even outside the ideal cluster. Lead evaluation and build the business case.
Approach: demo-driven, calculated ROI, seniority-based outreach.
📡 Champion C — Peripheral
Managers, Senior Managers, unidentified roles in peripheral clusters. Insufficient seniority to influence purchase — end-user or field profile. Awareness only.
Approach: passive nurture — no direct Sales outreach.
